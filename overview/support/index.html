 User Support

We are trying our best to support users of the Yarra Framework, but unfortunately our time is limited since we are a very small group. Please use our Gitter channel to send us your questions and requests. This is the most effective way for getting in touch with us and ensures that everyone from the Yarra team can answer your post. We also would like to encourage other members from the community to support users with questions on Yarra through the channel.

Gitter is a free messaging service operated by GitLab and requires only a GitHub or Twitter account to participate.
